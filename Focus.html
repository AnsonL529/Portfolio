A Glimpse of
the product

FocusPaw aims to support students with ADHD
and enhance their learning experience through
personalized, interactive tools.

Recording and AI Transcript Generation:
Enables users, especially ADHD students,
to capture key information

Gamified Self-Assessment:
Game-based learning promotes independent
learning and tracks progress effectively

Recap Feature:
Allows ADHD students to catch up on missed
information to ensure they stay on track

User Journey

1. In class

Recording and Transcription: FocusPaw connects to
the teacher's microphone to record and transcribe the
lesson content.

Break Feature: When students find it hard to concentrate,
they can click the break button.

Content Segmentation and Visuals: The AI-powered pet
segments the teacher material, while AI-generated images
enhance understanding and engagement for ADHD students.

2. Self-test

Gamified Self-Assessment: Game-based learning
promotes independent learning and tracks progress
effectively

3. Lesson Recap

Recap Feature: Allows ADHD students to catch up on
missed information to ensure they stay on track
